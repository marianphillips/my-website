About Me

I joined a bootcamp (Boolean UK), where I went from the basics to being able to develop my own full stack app. Please see my portfolio page for more details.

Outside of coding, I have many hobbies including netball, swimming, climbing, cycling, skiing, travel, reading, film, cabaret/circus and many more!
